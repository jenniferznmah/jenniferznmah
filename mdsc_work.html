Volunteer Work at Mood Disorders Society of Canada

I recently volunteered as a Website Auditor at Mood Disorders Society of Canada. I worked on the Mood Disorders Society of Canada English website and the Defeat Depression campaign website.

My role was to review and edit the websites for grammar and spelling errors and broken hyperlinks. I also checked the website for responsiveness issues and made recommendations to improve the website flow. I did other duties as assigned. Everything was reported to the National Program Manager.
